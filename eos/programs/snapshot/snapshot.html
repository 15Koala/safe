Attention

You are presently generating a snapshot based on variables used to generate official testnet snapshot. However, because EOS ERC20 tokens are liquid on the Ethereum network, and not frozen like they will be for mainnet snapshot, your snapshot will differ from the snapshot.csv committed to the EOSIO/genesis repository. This is because ethereum balances of registrants are susceptible to change at anytime while tokens are liquid.

This interface is provided for experimentation and verification purposes only, and should not be utilized to generate a testnet genesis.json
